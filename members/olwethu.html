Meet Olwethu

Biography

PROJECT OFFICER: HLONGWANE OLWETHU Olwethu is the current Projects Officer at IkusasaLentsha and a passionate second-year student pursuing a BSc in Agriculture (Agronomy) at the University of Zululand. Before stepping into the project’s role, Olwethu served as the organization’s Sponsorship Director for a full year. During that time, they played a key role in securing support and resources to advance the organization's mission. With a strong sense of responsibility and a heart for community upliftment, Olwethu brings both experience and energy to every project. Now, as Projects Officer, Olwethu continues to work closely with the team to plan, coordinate, and implement impactful initiatives that support learners and youth in under-resourced communities. With a background rooted in agriculture and a deep commitment to sustainable development, Olwethu is driven to make a difference—both in education and beyond.
