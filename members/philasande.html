Meet Philasande

Biography

Philasande is a passionate and driven student at Mangosuthu University of Technology, where he is pursuing a qualification in Information Technology, specializing in Network Engineering. He became a part of the Ikusasalentsha family in September 2023 and has taken on the role of Head of Communications in December 2024. In this role, Philasande is responsible for engaging with the public through the organization’s social media platforms (Facebook, TikTok and Instagram) ensuring that followers are informed, inspired, and involved. His dedication to clear and consistent communication helps maintain a strong connection between the organization and the communities it serves. With a heart for service and a mind for technology, Philasande blends his IT skills with his passion for outreach. His commitment to growth, collaboration, and community upliftment continues to make a positive difference within Ikusasalentsha and beyond.
