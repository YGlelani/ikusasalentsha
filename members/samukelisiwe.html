Biography

Samukelisiwe leads a role of being a Sponsorship director at Ikusasalentsha and with an unquenchable thirst for scientific innovation and a strong tech-savvy edge, Thando masterfully forges strategic partnerships that drive the organization’s mission forward. Passionate, driven, and focused on making a real impact, Thando brings energy and vision to everything they do. Thando joined the team in 2025, initially to assist with bursary applications. However, their passion and commitment quickly stood out—earning them a promotion to lead sponsorship initiatives. In a nonprofit organization where securing funding is vital, Thando is fully dedicated to driving growth and expanding impact through powerful, strategic collaborations.
