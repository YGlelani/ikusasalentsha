Meet Tsebiso

Biography

I am Tsebiso Mohlakoana, an Information and Communications Technology (ICT) student at the Durban University of Technology. I have a strong passion for software engineering and innovative problem-solving. Since 2024, I have been an active member of Ikusasalentsha Organization, where I served as Secretary in 2024 and currently hold the position of Deputy Chairperson. I am committed to continuous learning and using technology to develop impactful solutions that contribute to the growth and success of the organization and the community it serves.
