Meet Mthobisi

Biography

Mthobisi Maphalala is a dedicated and driven individual with a strong passion for supporting youth development and education. An alumnus of the University of the Free State, he graduated with a degree in Agricultural Management. Since becoming an active member in 2022, Mthobisi served as the head of the sponsorships from 2022 to 2023. In 2025 he is serving as a chairperson, where he leverages his advocacy and leadership skills to ensure the organization remains focused on its mission.
