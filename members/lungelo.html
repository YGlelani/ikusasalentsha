Meet Lungelo

Biography

Lungelo Zikode is the Deputy Chairperson of Ikusasalentsha. She served as Chairperson from 2021 to 2023, driven by a strong passion for helping learners achieve their goals. Her commitment to youth development has deepened over the years as she actively contributes to initiatives that provide mentorship, educational support, and opportunities for personal growth. In addition to her role at Ikusasalentsha, Lungelo has a background in Textile Sciences and currently works as a Laboratory Technician at Pro Textile. She is dedicated to making a meaningful impact in the community. Lungelo's leadership and dedication continue to inspire those around her, fostering a culture of empowerment and positive change within the organization.
