Meet Xolani

Biography

Xolani Mazibuko is the Secretary of IkusasaLentsha Organization, where he coordinates programs that empower youth and drive socio‑economic development in the community. He holds a Bachelor of Environmental Health degree from the University of Johannesburg, which informs his approach to health‑related projects and sustainable initiatives. He has a deep passion for helping young people access education. He dedicates his time to mentoring learners from disadvantaged backgrounds, empowering them to pursue higher education. In 2024, Xolani joined Ikusasalentsha, a community organization focused on socio‑economic development. He quickly rose through the ranks, becoming Deputy Secretary in December 2024. Through his work, Xolani aims to make a positive impact in his hometown of Bergville and contribute to the eradication of poverty. "Together, we strive to create lasting change and inspire the next generation" (Mazibuko,2026).
