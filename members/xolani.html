Meet Xolani

Biography

Xolani is a University of Johannesburg student with a deep passion for helping young people access education. He dedicates his time to tutoring and guiding students from disadvantaged backgrounds, empowering them to pursue higher education. In 2024, Xolani joined Ikusasalentsha, a community organization focused on socio-economic development. He quickly rose through the ranks, becoming Deputy Secretary in December 2024. Through his work, Xolani aims to make a positive impact in his hometown of Bergville and contribute to the eradication of poverty.
