Meet Aphelele

Biography

Aphelele is a dedicated and hardworking student at Durban University of Technology, pursuing a degree in ICT: Business Analysis. He recently completed an Industry Training Course, enhancing his expertise in the ICT/IT sector. Aphelele joined Ikusasalentsha in 2023, having received support from the organization during his high school years. After serving as Deputy Secretary for two years, he was appointed Secretary, where he continues to make a positive impact. Passionate about helping and uplifting those around him, Aphelele firmly believes that teamwork makes the dream work. Additionally, he is involved in a student-led organization that donates food and clothes to orphanages in Durban, further showcasing his commitment to helping others.
