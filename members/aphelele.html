Meet Aphelele

Biography

Aphelele is a dedicated, hardworking, and passionate individual committed to continuous growth and community development. He is an alumnus of the Durban University of Technology, where he completed his qualification in ICT. He furthered his academic journey by enrolling for an Advanced Diploma in ICT, demonstrating his strong commitment to expanding his expertise in the technology field. He also successfully completed an Industry Training Course, equipping him with valuable practical experience within the IT sector. He has been an active member since 2023. Upon joining, he immediately served as an executive member from 2023 to date under the office of the secretary, serving diligently and dedicated. In this role, he continues to contribute strategically and administratively to advance the organization’s mission and impact within the community.
