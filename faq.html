Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

IkusasaLentsha is a non-profit organization based in Bergville, KwaZulu-Natal, founded in October 2020 by Ms. Nomfundo Nkosi. It focuses on community development and education, helping rural learners access university applications, bursaries, career guidance, and professional development opportunities.

IkusasaLentsha is based in Bergville, KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa.

Our mission is to help rural learners achieve their educational and career goals by providing access to resources, mentorship, and opportunities.

We provide assistance with university applications, bursary information, career guidance, exam support, mentorship programs, and skill development workshops.

Our exclusive partner is the Durban University of Technology (DUT), and we collaborate with organizations like Love Life and the Department of Social Development (DSD).

Donations can be made through our official channels. Visit our "Donate" page for details on bank transfers or partnerships.

You can contact us via email, phone, or through our social media platforms such as Facebook.
