Sanitary Pads Campaign

Periods shouldn’t be a barrier to education. Our Sanitary Pads Campaign supports rural schoolgirls by ensuring they have access to essential menstrual products—so they can attend school with dignity, confidence, and comfort.

What We Do:

  • Distribute sanitary pads to learners in rural schools
  • Provide menstrual health education during handover events
  • Promote community awareness around menstrual hygiene and gender equality

Annual Impact:

Now in its third year, this campaign has reached hundreds of girls through events hosted at schools such as Ekwaluseni, Potshini, and Mafu High School. Each year, we aim to expand our reach to even more learners.

Why It Matters:

Many girls in rural areas miss school due to lack of access to sanitary products. Our campaign fights period poverty, reduces absenteeism, and supports SDG 5: Gender Equality and the right to education for all girls.

Highlights:

  • 2023–2025: Over 500 girls supported
  • Annual Menstrual Health Events
  • Support from local community leaders and donors
