Ikusasalentsha Career Elevation Program

Graduation is just the beginning. Our Career Elevation Program supports Ikusasalentsha graduates in taking the next step—from the classroom to the workplace. This program is designed to boost employability, confidence, and career readiness for rural youth.

What We Offer:

  • CV & Cover Letter Writing Workshops
  • Mock Interviews to prepare for real job opportunities
  • Networking Sessions to build professional connections
  • Peer Mentorship and support groups for job-seeking graduates

Strategic Partnerships:

In partnership with the Durban University of Technology (DUT) Career Resource Centre, we equip graduates with real-world insights, tools, and confidence to succeed in competitive job markets.

Program Goals:

  • Close the gap between education and employment
  • Empower graduates to pursue careers aligned with their goals
  • Promote peer-driven support and collaboration among young professionals

Why It Matters:

Rural graduates often face limited access to career guidance and job-prep support. This program ensures they’re not navigating the world of work alone—we walk the journey with them.
