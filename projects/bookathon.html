Bergville Literacy Program

The Bergville Literacy Program is one of IKusasalentsha's core initiatives aimed at building a strong reading culture in rural primary schools. Through creative and engaging literacy activities, the program develops learners' skills in reading, writing, public speaking, and critical thinking.

  • Spelling Bees
  • Skits & Storytelling Performances
